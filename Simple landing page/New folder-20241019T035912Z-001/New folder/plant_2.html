🌿 Plant Care Information 🌿

🌼 Season: Spring-Summer
☀️ Sunlight: Full Sun/Partial Shade
🌱 Soil: Light & Well-drained
💧 Water: Regular, Not Soggy
🌡️ Temperature: 20°C to 30°C

🌿 Plantation Steps:

  1. Pick a sunny or partly shady spot.
  2. Plant the bulb 3-4 inches deep in loose soil.
  3. Water regularly to keep the soil slightly moist.
  4. Spread mulch around the plant to keep the soil cool and moist.
  5. Feed with fertilizer every 2-3 months.
  6. Cut dead leaves and flowers to keep the plant healthy.
